Alex landed hard on a wooden floor. He was aware of the new surroundings; he wasn't in the playground anymore. Instead he was inside a dimly lit eerie house.

The air was dry around him, the walls were covered in scratches and mysterious stains. A door in his sight creaked open on it's own. Alex, now scared, had two choices to make.

Will Alex go upstairs, or will he go down to the basement?
