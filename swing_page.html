Alex moved carefully into the secluded area, approaching the swings with caution.

He hesitated to approach them, but then he gave one a push.

Nothing happened at first, but then a shadowy figure came behind him suddenly, just out of sight.

Alex was starting to feel uneasy; he stepped back and looked toward the slide, wanting to investigate further.
