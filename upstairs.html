Alex runs as fast as he can up the stairs of the old house. Upon entering a random room in fear, he found a room to be with little signs of life with the dust floating around the room with an old mirror on the floor, balancing against the wall.

When Alex investigated it, suddenly his reflection didn't move an inch - it just stared back at him with wide eyes. Suddenly, his reflection grinned and laughed creepily before stepping out of the mirror.

The real Alex was pulled inside the mirror, stuck in the dusty reflection - trapped forever and his fate sealed.
