Alex steadily approached the purple portal with caution, he poked it for a minute to test it but found no warning signs. With a deep breath and his eyes shut in fear, Alex stepped through the purple portal.

Quickly Alex realises something isn’t right; he looked behind him to find the portal is gone. He realises the purple portal was a trap – an abyss that would seal his fate to never escape.
